*March 9, 2026 — Felix*

## What happened
On March 2, 2026, Intuit and Anthropic announced a partnership to bring custom AI agents to mid-market businesses. Key details:

- **Claude Agent SDK** integrated into Intuit platform
- Businesses can build and deploy **industry-specific AI agents** for compliant workflows
- Focused on **mid-market** (our exact target)
- Deep integration with Intuit products (QuickBooks, etc.)
- Sources: [Intuit IR](https://investors.intuit.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/1305/), [PYMNTS](https://www.pymnts.com/partnerships/2026/intuit-and-anthropic-to-launch-customizable-ai-agents/), [Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/intuit-anthropic-ai-agents-aim-220826650.html)

## Why this matters for Getmilo

### Threat
- **Direct competition in CPA vertical** — Intuit owns the accounting software stack. If they offer built-in AI agents, CPA firms may default to Intuit's solution.
- **Legitimizes the market** — but also brings enterprise-grade competition
- **Distribution advantage** — Intuit already has the customer relationship

### Opportunity
- **"Own everything" counter-positioning** — Intuit's agents will be platform-locked. Ours are one-time, open, vendor-independent.
- **Speed gap** — Intuit partnership is "announced" not "shipped." Enterprise rollouts take 6-12 months. We can be in market now.
- **Anti-vendor-lock narrative** — "Your accounting software vendor shouldn't control your AI too."
- **Independent accountants** — Many CPAs actively resist Intuit. They're our natural audience.
- **Blog content opportunity** — "Intuit is building AI agents for your accountant. Here's why that should worry you."

## Recommended actions
1. **Write a blog post** positioning Milo as the independent, own-everything alternative to platform-locked AI agents (target keyword: "intuit ai agents alternative")
2. **Update outbound email hooks** — reference the Intuit announcement as urgency driver ("Intuit just announced AI agents for your competitors. Don't wait for their rollout — be 6 months ahead.")
3. **Target Intuit-skeptic CPA communities** — there are active Reddit and forum communities of accountants who prefer independent tools

## Priority: HIGH — Time-sensitive (news is 7 days old, window for thought leadership content is ~2-3 weeks)
