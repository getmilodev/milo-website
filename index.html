SPEC:005 // why milo

Most firms have AI tabs open. Very few have AI systems their team can actually run.

The market keeps selling "use AI better." That is not specific enough. The real drag lives in proposal turnaround, client reporting, follow-up, and internal research that still depend on whoever remembers to do them.

Milo is strongest when the buyer already uses AI a little, but not in a dependable way. We take that scattered usage, rebuild it into a workflow your team can inherit, and leave behind a system instead of a black box.

A typical first system ships in 2-5 days. Your ongoing cost after delivery is still mostly hosting and API usage, not a new monthly vendor bill. That only matters if the handoff is clean, which is why we treat documentation and training as part of the product.
