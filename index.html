SPEC:005 // why milo

Operator-first AI beats vendor-first AI

We are not trying to convince you AI matters. We assume you already know that. Milo exists for the messy middle: the team already experimenting with AI, but without a dependable operating layer behind it.

That is why the work starts with concrete workflows operators care about: proposals, reporting, follow-up, and research. Not vague transformation decks. Not generic chatbot demos.

You keep ownership of the system after delivery. That matters because useful AI should become embedded in how the firm runs, not rented from a vendor forever.
