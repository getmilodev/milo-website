## 3. Algorithm Mechanics — What Actually Matters (2026)

### The Ranking Formula
| Factor | Weight | What To Do |
|--------|--------|------------|
| **Comments** | **~40-50× an upvote** | Reply to EVERY comment. Ask follow-ups. Each unanswered comment costs ~5 ranking positions after hour 3. |
| **Upvote velocity** | More important than total count | Sustained 25-50 upvotes/hr beats a spike. 500 steady beats 600 with dead periods. |
| **First-hour engagement** | **~4× later hours** | Need 20-50 supporters with active PH accounts in first 60 min. |
| **Account quality** | Heavy filtering | Votes from new/inactive accounts are discounted or filtered entirely. One founder got 100+ votes, stuck at #26 all day — all from fresh accounts. |
| **Comment depth** | Strong positive signal | Multi-reply threads > single comments. |

### What KILLS Launches
- ❌ 20+ upvotes in first 10 minutes (triggers manual review)
- ❌ Comment-to-upvote ratio below 1:20 (signals manipulation)
- ❌ Votes from accounts created within 7 days of launch (low/zero weight)
- ❌ One massive outreach blast (unnatural spike → flagged)
- ❌ Going dark for hours (unanswered comments tank ranking)
- ❌ Generic comments ("Great project!") — don't help, may hurt
- ❌ Asking for upvotes directly (against rules, gets flagged)

### What WINS
- ✅ Staggered outreach in 4-5 timed waves (2-3× better than single blast)
- ✅ Maker comment posted within 60 seconds of launch (85%+ correlation with top 10)
- ✅ Reply time under 15 minutes to every comment
- ✅ PH-exclusive deal (drives engagement + comments)
- ✅ Using **Shoutouts** feature to mention tools used in building (Twilio, Anthropic, Vercel → their teams may reshare, PH official account may pick up)
- ✅ Asking **specific** questions ("What integrations matter most?" not "What do you think?")
- ✅ 70% of #1 products included a structured first comment

### Benchmark Data (2026 launches)
- **Dub.co** — #1 Day/Week/Month: 1,085 upvotes, 210 comments, 2,000+ visitors, 663 signups. Thursday. No video. Used Shoutouts feature.
- **Tally 2.0** — #1 + Golden Kitty: 1,000+ upvotes. Weekly signups 2,300 → 4,400 (+91%). Now $3M+ ARR with 5-person team.
- **Plausible** — #2: 850 upvotes, minimal prep. 1,490 visitors, only 22 trial signups (1.38% conversion). Called PH "nice to have."
- **Screen Studio** — Product of Year finalist: 8,000 customers in 9 months post-launch. Best sales day ($3,467) came months AFTER PH — not from PH itself.

**Pattern:** Every successful launch had (1) an existing audience to activate, (2) a product that solves an obvious problem, or (3) relentless comment engagement. Nobody got lucky with zero preparation.
