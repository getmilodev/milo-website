LAND:002.2 // the tax season capacity problem

The Tax Season Capacity Problem

Every CPA firm knows the pattern. January through April, the workload explodes. Your team is buried in returns, K-1s, and extension requests. Meanwhile, new client inquiries pile up, document collection requests go unanswered, and follow-up falls through the cracks.

Every time a preparer stops to chase a missing document or answer "what do I need to bring to my appointment," they lose 15-20 minutes of focused work. Every inquiry that sits unanswered risks losing a new client worth $2,800 in recurring annual revenue.

Most firms solve this by hiring seasonal staff. But training people who leave in April is expensive, inconsistent, and exhausting. And no temp handles the 9pm panic from a business owner who just realized they're missing a W-2. The problem is not headcount — it's that the workflows themselves are manual.

LAND:002.3 // capabilities

What AI Native Does for Your Firm

We find the workflow that bleeds the most time and make it dependable. For most accounting firms, that starts with document collection and client intake — then expands to follow-up sequences, reporting cycles, and deadline management.

  • 01
    Document collection that stays on track
    W-2s, 1099s, K-1s, bank statements — the system tracks what's outstanding per client, sends reminders on a cadence, and flags what's overdue. Your preparers stop being document chasers.
  • 02
    Client intake that qualifies and schedules
    New inquiry comes in at 8pm? The intake workflow captures what they need (personal return, business, bookkeeping, payroll), their timeline, and whether it's a good fit. You get a structured summary before you call back.
  • 03
    Follow-up that doesn't depend on someone remembering
    New client inquiry sat for three days? The system follows up. Engagement letter sent but not signed? Reminder goes out. Appointment booked but not confirmed? Another reminder. No client falls through because the next step was on someone's mental to-do list.
  • 04
    Reporting that assembles itself
    Client status dashboards, preparation pipeline, outstanding documents by client — the system pulls the data and drafts the report. No more rebuilding the same tracking spreadsheet every Monday morning.
  • 05
    Deadline management that doesn't live in someone's head
    Extension deadlines, estimated payment dates, engagement renewals — tracked, reminded, and escalated. April 14th panic messages get a clear, factual response even if nobody on your team is available.

LAND:002.5 // real scenarios

Real Scenarios

March 12th, 7:30pm — New client inquiry

A small business owner Googles "CPA near me" and fills out your contact form. Your office closed at 5.

Without AI Native: The inquiry sits in your inbox until tomorrow. They reach out to two more firms. Someone responds first. You lost a client worth $4,000/year in recurring fees.

With AI Native: The intake workflow captures their business type and needs, qualifies the fit, and queues a follow-up. You get a notification: "New client inquiry — LLC, restaurant, needs business tax + bookkeeping." Next morning, you call with context instead of starting cold.

February 20th — 14 clients still missing documents

Your team has 14 clients who haven't sent their W-2s or 1099s. Three preparers are heads-down on returns. Nobody has time to follow up.

Without AI Native: A preparer stops working to send 14 individual follow-up emails. Forty minutes of focused time lost. Two clients still don't respond. The returns get pushed back.

With AI Native: The document collection workflow has been tracking all 14 automatically. Reminders went out at day 7 and day 14. Three clients uploaded their documents after the second reminder. The remaining 11 got escalated to your team with a summary of what's still outstanding. Nobody stopped working to chase.

April 14th, 9pm — Deadline panic

An existing client messages, frantic. "Did you file my extension? I just realized tomorrow is the deadline."

Without AI Native: No response until morning. Client spirals. Texts your personal number at 10pm. You spend 20 minutes reassuring them.

With AI Native: The system responds with the facts: "Extensions for all clients were filed on April 10th. You're all set. Would you like a confirmation email?" Client calms down. You sleep through the night.
