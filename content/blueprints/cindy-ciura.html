Your consulting workflow today

Each step shows what's happening now and what changes with Milo.

Now

New client signs. Someone sends a welcome email with a list of things needed. Half the information trickles in over weeks. The team starts work with incomplete context. Onboarding feels like it happens by accident.

With Milo

Structured onboarding flow captures brand assets, login credentials, goals, and constraints before kickoff. Auto-confirms receipt of each item. Team walks into the first working session with everything they need.

Now

Every proposal starts from scratch or from a half-remembered template. Scoping takes hours of back-and-forth. By the time you send the proposal, the prospect has talked to two other agencies.

With Milo

Proposal system pulls from past work, the client brief, and your scope templates to generate a solid first draft. You edit and personalize instead of building from zero. Turnaround drops from days to hours.

Now

Work lives in Slack, email, spreadsheets, and someone's head. Deadlines drift. Team capacity is invisible until someone burns out. The client finds out about delays when the deliverable is late.

With Milo

Centralized project tracking with automatic status updates. Capacity visible across the team. Bottlenecks flagged before they become fires. Clients see progress without asking.

Now

End-of-month reporting is a manual scramble — pulling data from multiple platforms, formatting slides, writing summaries. It takes a full day and nobody enjoys it.

With Milo

Automated reports pull real performance data, formatted to your brand template. Generated on schedule and ready for review. Your team adds strategic insights instead of assembling numbers.

Now

Clients email asking for updates. Your team context-switches to answer status questions. Scheduling calls is back-and-forth. Important messages get buried in threads.

With Milo

Automated project status updates at each milestone. Scheduling links in every communication. Your team focuses on the work, not the inbox.
