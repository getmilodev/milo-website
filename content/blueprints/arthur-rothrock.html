BLUEPRINT:002 // the real problem

What most firms miss

The billable hour anxiety you described comes from uncertainty — every task feels like a guess. When the admin work (intake, document management, client follow-up) eats unpredictable chunks of time, the billable hours get squeezed and the stress compounds.

Automating the non-billable operational work gives you cleaner time blocks for actual legal work. The billable hours become more predictable because there's less noise around them.

BLUEPRINT:003 // current state

Your law firm workflow today

Each step shows what's happening now and what changes with Milo.

Now

Potential client calls or emails. Someone takes notes by hand or in a basic form. Half the information is missing — you spend the first meeting collecting what should have been gathered upfront. No conflict check until you're already invested.

With Milo

Structured intake form captures case type, key dates, opposing parties, and documents before the first meeting. Auto-runs conflict check. Data flows into your case management system. You walk into the first meeting fully prepared.

Now

Clients email documents in batches — medical records mixed with insurance correspondence mixed with personal notes. Your team manually sorts, names, and files everything. Missing documents surface weeks later.

With Milo

Document upload portal with case-specific checklists. AI classifies and organizes files by type. Missing items flagged automatically with client-specific follow-up. Your paralegal reviews organized files instead of building the organization from scratch.

Now

Key dates live in multiple places — the calendar, the case file, someone's memory. Status updates require checking multiple systems. Deadlines occasionally slip through.

With Milo

Centralized case dashboard with automated deadline tracking. Status changes trigger team notifications. Nothing lives in someone's head — every case milestone is visible and tracked.

Now

Clients call asking "what's happening with my case?" Your team answers the same questions repeatedly. Scheduling meetings is back-and-forth over email.

With Milo

Automated status updates at each case milestone. Scheduling links in every communication. Your team handles substantive questions, not status checks.

Now

Time entries pile up. End-of-month billing is a scramble. Invoices go out late. Clients dispute charges they don't understand because the descriptions are too vague.

With Milo

Time tracking nudges keep entries current. Invoices auto-generate with clear, client-friendly descriptions. Billing happens on schedule, not whenever someone gets around to it.
