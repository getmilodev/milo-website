BLUEPRINT:002 // the real problem

What most firms miss

You're spending real time getting new hires up to speed on how your firm actually works -- which has almost nothing to do with what they learned in school. Every new graduate is a training project before they're productive, and that training load falls on you.

The workflows that eat the most training time are the same ones that eat the most staff time: intake, document processing, client follow-up. Systematize those first, and new hires ramp faster because there's less tribal knowledge to transfer -- the process is built into the system.

BLUEPRINT:003 // current state

Your client intake workflow today

Each step shows what's happening now and what changes with Milo.

Now

Inquiry arrives by email or voicemail. Someone manually copies details into a spreadsheet or CRM. Half the time the info is incomplete — you chase the rest by phone.

With Milo

A structured intake form captures everything upfront — entity type, filing needs, timeline. Auto-confirms receipt. Data flows directly into your system. No manual entry, no chasing.

Now

Clients email PDFs in random batches. Some use the portal, which breaks. Others drop off paper. Your team manually checks what's missing, then follows up by email or phone. Repeat 3-4 times per client.

With Milo

A document checklist tied to client type auto-generates at intake. Clients upload to a single link. AI flags missing docs and sends a specific follow-up — "We still need your 1099-NEC" — without your team touching it.

Now

Staff downloads attachments, renames files, creates folders, and manually enters figures into your prep software. This step alone eats 20-30 minutes per client.

With Milo

Uploaded documents are auto-classified, renamed by type, and organized into the client folder. AI extracts key figures (W-2 wages, 1099 amounts) into a prep summary your team reviews instead of building from scratch.

Now

Clients email asking "where's my return?" Your team answers the same questions repeatedly. Scheduling review calls is back-and-forth over email.

With Milo

Automated status updates at each stage ("Documents received", "In review", "Ready for signature"). Scheduling link in every communication. Your team only handles actual questions, not status checks.

Now

This part mostly works — it's the professional work you're trained for. But you arrive here exhausted from the four steps above.

With Milo

Same professional review, but you start with a clean, pre-organized file and a client who's been kept informed throughout. E-sign and delivery are one click. You spend your time on judgment, not logistics.
