# Competitive Intelligence: Done-For-You AI Agent Setup Services
*Updated: March 9, 2026 | Category: Direct competitors (setup services, not SaaS)*

## Context

These are companies selling done-for-you AI agent setup — our exact service category. Different from the AI receptionist SaaS competitors (Trillet, Ring Eden, etc.) tracked in `competitive-update-march9.md`. These sell the *build*, not the *subscription*.

## Competitor Breakdown

### 1. QuickSetupAI (quicksetupai.com)
- **Run by:** Morgan Harris (solopreneur)
- **Pricing:**
  - Setup: $499 one-time (Custom GPT + 1-2 automations + docs)
  - Maintenance: $79/mo (prompt tuning, minor tweaks, priority support)
  - Growth: $699 setup + $99/mo (setup + maintenance + quarterly roadmap)
- **Delivery:** 5 business days
- **Focus:** Custom GPTs, workflow automation (Zapier-style), email triage
- **Sales process:** Calendly booking → audit → build → maintain
- **Site quality:** Clean, professional, single-page
- **Our advantage:** We're $100 cheaper on setup ($399 vs $499). We don't charge monthly ($0 vs $79-99/mo). We build coordinated multi-agent teams, not single GPTs. We run 12 agents in production — authentic credibility they can't match.
- **Their advantage:** They include monthly maintenance by default. Some buyers want ongoing hand-holding.

### 2. Custom Built Agents (custombuiltagents.net)
- **Run by:** Taylor Cox, Modesto CA (solopreneur)
- **Pricing:**
  - Quick Setup: $175 (basic OpenClaw + 1 platform + 14 days support)
  - Custom Build: $260 (custom skills/personality + multi-platform + 30 days)
  - Full Ops: $435 (everything + trading bots + social automation + 60 days)
- **Delivery:** 1-2 hours (live screen-share session)
- **Focus:** OpenClaw specifically, heavy on trading bots (Kalshi) and crypto
- **Claims:** 150+ agents built, 975% trading ROI
- **Sales process:** Telegram bot → discovery call → live build session
- **Site quality:** Flashy, crypto-bro aesthetic, lots of emojis
- **Our advantage:** Professional positioning vs their crypto/trading focus. We serve real businesses (dental, legal, HVAC) not day traders. Multi-agent coordination vs single agent setup. Better site, better content, better brand.
- **Their advantage:** Cheaper ($175-435 vs $399-2,499). Faster (1-2 hours vs 2-5 days). Lower barrier to entry.

## Strategic Takeaway

The "done-for-you AI agent setup" market is emerging with small solopreneurs. Nobody has scale yet. Our advantages:
1. **Multi-agent teams** — nobody else does this
2. **No monthly fees** — QuickSetupAI charges $79-99/mo ongoing
3. **Professional brand** — 47-page site vs single-page competitors
4. **Authentic credibility** — 12 agents in production daily

The risk: if a well-funded player enters this space with marketing budget, the window narrows. Speed matters.
