# Partnership Channels — Executive Referrals
*Created: 2026-03-09*

## Thesis

Fractional executives and business operating system implementers are natural referral partners for Milo's AI for Executives product. They serve the same audience (50-250 person companies), are trusted advisors, and benefit when their clients adopt AI (makes their own fractional work more efficient).

## Channel 1: Fractional Executive Communities

### Fractional Connections Community (fractionals.ai)
- **What:** Community of fractional leaders in Ops, Marketing, Sales, Revenue, HR, Tech, Strategy
- **Why it matters:** Members explicitly cross-refer clients. A fractional COO who recommends AI enablement to their client is a warm introduction.
- **Model:** Join community → build relationships → offer referral fee for /executives clients
- **URL:** https://fractionals.ai/fccommunity/

### Top Fractional COO Firms
- **NeoGig** (yourneogig.com) — curated fractional exec network
- **Toptal** — large platform with fractional COO placements
- **Aiken House** — fractional COO advisory (aikenhouse.com)
- **Model:** Partner program → fractional COOs offer AI enablement as an add-on to their advisory

### Why This Works
- Forbes (Dec 2025): "Companies of all sizes will build entire operating models around fractional strategy"
- Fractional work statistics show referrals are the #1 client acquisition channel
- A fractional COO saying "let me introduce you to the team that made me AI-native" is extremely high-trust

## Channel 2: EOS Implementers

### EOS Worldwide (eosworldwide.com)
- **What:** ~500+ certified EOS implementers in the US who run quarterly sessions with leadership teams
- **Target:** The "Integrator" role in EOS companies (equivalent to COO)
- **Directory:** implementer.eosworldwide.com
- **Model:** Identify implementers serving professional services firms → pitch co-delivery or referral partnership
- **Barrier:** EOS is a structured system; adding AI enablement would need to complement, not compete

## Channel 3: Executive Coaching Platforms

### Vistage / YPO / EO
- **What:** CEO/executive peer advisory groups (16-20 members per group)
- **Why it matters:** Members share vendor recommendations. One successful engagement could cascade to 15+ referrals.
- **Model:** Get one member AI-native → they become an evangelist in their peer group
- **Barrier:** Expensive/slow — these groups are invite-only and trust-based

## Recommended First Move

1. **Join Fractional Connections Community** — lowest barrier, most aligned audience
2. **Identify 5 fractional COOs** who serve professional services firms (50-200 employees)
3. **Offer a pilot:** Free AI enablement session for the fractional COO themselves → they experience the value firsthand → they refer clients
4. **Formalize:** 10-15% referral fee on /executives engagements ($200-$750 per referral)

## Revenue Model

- 5 fractional COO partners → each refers 1 client/quarter
- 5 clients/quarter × $3,500 avg = $17,500/quarter = **$70,000/year**
- Partners earn $350-$525 per referral (10-15%)
- **ROI:** High. Zero ad spend. Trust-based pipeline.
